Review Of Bulgarian Design is a series of exhibitions produced by Komplekt since 2018 as a highlight of Melba Design Festival. The subjective selection of distinctive examples from the last year in a variety of fields is an important part of the collective memory of the state of Bulgarian visual culture. Komplekt sets a specific topic, invites a well-known curator or collective to navigate and reflect on the process, and produces new design objects together with selected artists.
